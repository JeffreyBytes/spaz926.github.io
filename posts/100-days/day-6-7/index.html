100 Days of Swift: Days 6 & 7

March 5, 2019 One minute read
100 Days of Swift • Swift • Education
swift • education

Day 6 was Swift Review, Day Three covering the topics of properties, static properties, methods, access control, closures, polymorphism, and typecasting. Like days 4 and 5, just reviewing swift fundamentals.

Day 7 marks the beginning of Project 1, a basic image viewing application. Fundamentally it shows a list of image names in a table view, and when the user taps on an image name it shows the image on a new screen.

Today was just setting up the project, creating a new TableView, and showing the image names in the table. The list of image names is retrieved using FileManger and saved as an array of strings.

All very basic stuff, but important to review because table views are used in many iOS applications.
