100 Days of Swift: Days 10 & 11

March 9, 2019 One minute read
100 Days of Swift • Swift • Education
swift • education

Days 10 & 11 have been two very straight forward days building Project 2, a flag guessing game. The app shows three random country flags on the screen and tracks the user’s score.

It incorporates the use of Auto Layout, useful built-in methods of Int and Array, and introduces UIAlertController, @IBOutlet, and @IBAction.

It’s a simple application, but allowed me to practice some importatnt aspects of developing for iOS. The UI is a bit lackluster, and I will be playing around with it to get the right feel before tomorrow’s challenges.
